Earn WMTx by powering the World Mobile network

  • Globally, there are nearly 3 billion people unconnected to mobile networks. In the USA, 24 million people still live in digital deserts, without access to internet connectivity.

  • They remain unconnected, because legacy mobile network operators are struggling to meet the growing demand for connectivity.

  • By leveraging blockchain, crypto and decentralised wireless infrastructure, World Mobile provides the solution to the challenge of meeting the continually increasing demand for mobile network coverage globally.

How WMTx powers the World Mobile network:

1.

World Mobile customers pay for calls, texts, and data using local currency creating network revenue.

2.

EarthNode operators and stakers earn WMTx for processing network transactions and services for customers.

3.

World Mobile uses the network revenue to purchase WMTx from open markets.

4.

Operators and stakers are allocated their rewards that World Mobile purchased from open markets.

5.

The supply of WMTx is fixed even as the network grows.
